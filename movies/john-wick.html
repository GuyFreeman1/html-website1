John Wick

With the untimely death of his beloved wife still bitter in his mouth, John Wick,

the expert former assassin, receives one final gift from her--a precious keepsake to help John find a new meaning in life

now that she is gone.

But when the arrogant Russian mob prince, Iosef Tarasov, and his men pay Wick a rather unwelcome visit to rob him of his prized 1969 Mustang and his wife's present,

the legendary hitman will be forced to unearth his meticulously concealed identity.
