The Hangover

Angelenos Doug Billings and Tracy Garner are about to get married.

Two days before the wedding, the four men in the wedding party - Doug,

Doug's two best buddies Phil Wenneck and Stu Price, and Tracy's brother Alan Garner - hop into Tracy's father's

beloved Mercedes convertible for a 24-hour stag party to Las Vegas. Phil, a married high school teacher,

has the same maturity level as his students when he's with his pals. Stu, a dentist, is worried about everything,

especially what his controlling girlfriend Melissa thinks.
